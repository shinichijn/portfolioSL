About Me

Sherry Lang is an artist and independent designer living and working in Los Angeles, CA. It is my passioned of photography, design and style to craft together beautifully creative stories that can capture an vivid and living world.

The range of projects that I design and collaborate on is anywhere from print to digital, to textile to product. I’m always looking to try something new and innovated.
